We will be taking a 2 hour private charter around the bay on the Triton Charters catamaran. A cash bar is available for drinks. Pizza and cake will be provided for lunch. Please, no gifts!

Parking

There is no on-site parking. Triton is a short walk down the promenade from the public parking lot located at the corner of Shelter Island Drive & Anchorage Ln. (in front of the Brigantine Restaurant). Part of the lot is metered (up to 3 hours- quarters only), and the back portion of the lot is free. There is also surrounding street parking.

Alternatively, there is a large lot with free parking next to Bali Hai but is about 3/4 of a mile walk to the boat.

Waiver

RSVP must be made by November 9th (72 hours) before boat launch. The charter company requires your full name that is on your driver's license or other form of identification and to sign the waiver before boarding.
